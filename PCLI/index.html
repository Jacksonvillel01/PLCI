About

Introduction

Prayerline Church International (PCI) is a global Ghanaian-based Pentecostal denomination founded in 2016 by Apostle Dr. Joseph Kingsley Eshun.

Known for its strong emphasis on prayer, evangelism, discipleship, worship, and community impact, PCI has grown rapidly, establishing a strong presence in Ghana and beyond.

The church is dedicated to transforming lives through the power of Jesus Christ.


Vision

Changing lives through Jesus Christ.


Mission

To build a Christian family with a culture of soul-winning, praise, and worship.

To raise disciples with a passion for prayer and missions.

To extend a helping hand to the needy and outcasts in society.


History

PCI emerged from decades of midnight intercession, a mother’s persistent prayers for her sons, and a family’s unwavering obedience to divine guidance. Founded in 2016, the church has experienced rapid growth, establishing multiple branches and ministries in Ghana and internationally

Milestones

Growth & Church Planting: PCI has expanded with new branches across Ghana and envisions further global outreach.


Leadership Development: From one ordained minister at inception, PCI now boasts multiple trained leaders.


Annual Programs: Hosts impactful conferences like Missions Night and Worship & Warfare Conference to equip believers.


Spiritual Impact: Established the Jesus Prayer Garden, a dedicated prayer center in Western Ghana, attracting intercessors worldwide.


Community Impact: Engages in outreach programs, including orphanage visits, hospital ministries, and prison evangelism.


Core Values

A. Spiritual Foundations

Evangelism: Every member is called to preach Christ.
Discipleship: Training believers to emulate Christ’s character and works.
Missions: Raising and equipping missionaries to spread the gospel globally and plant churches.
Intercession: Developing prayer warriors to intercede for individuals, families, and nations.


B. Core Practices

Holy Spirit Baptism: Encouraging believers to seek the baptism of the Holy Spirit with speaking in tongues as initial evidence.
Fruit & Gifts of the Spirit: Cultivating spiritual gifts and Christ-like character.
Healing & Deliverance: Praying for the afflicted as part of salvation ministry.
Prayer: Emphasizing worship, supplication, and intercession.
Bible Teaching: Prioritizing scriptural truth and scripture memorization.


C. Ethical & Communal Values

Faithfulness & Integrity: Upholding moral excellence in all areas of life.
Respect for Authority: Honoring church leadership and divine order.
Mutual Respect: Fostering unity and dignity among members, regardless of background.
Sacrificial Service: Serving God and the church selflessly.
Tithes & Offering Faithfulness in financial stewardship to support ministry.
Holiness: Pursuing Christ-like purity in conduct.


D. Church Culture & Discipline

Praise & Worship: Cultivating a vibrant atmosphere of worship.
Communal Living: Encouraging fellowship, mutual support, and social responsibility.
Church Discipline: Maintaining order, accountability, and corrective measures in love.
Social Impact: Providing healthcare, education, and charity initiatives.


Founder

Apostle DR. Joseph Kingsley Eshun

Founder & General Overseer

Apostle DR. Joseph Kingsley Eshun is a visionary leader, author, and teacher of God's Word with over 25 years of ministry experience. He founded Prayerline Church International in 2005 with a divine mandate to raise prayer warriors and spread the gospel globally.

Under his leadership, the church has grown to over 50 branches across 3 continents, impacting millions of lives through various outreach programs and media ministries.
